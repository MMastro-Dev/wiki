# Career — Networking and Job Hunt (Months 6–9)

**Goal:** Get real market signal through targeted applications and intentional networking.
**Created:** 2026-05-28
**Status:** not-started

---

## Steps

### Preparation (month 6)

- [ ] Build a target list of 20 companies — weight toward AI-adjacent product companies and platform teams
- [ ] Update CV to final state — one page, quantified, positioning statement
- [ ] Start applying to 3–5 target companies as learning experiences
- [ ] Submit one OSS PR — candidates: mdBook plugins, Caddy plugins, React Hook Form, TanStack Query

### Networking (month 7)

- [ ] Outreach to 3–5 people at target companies (specific, reciprocal, not asking for referrals directly)
- [ ] Evaluate internal promotion conversation at AlmavivA — especially if Azure architecture exposure has materialised

### Interview prep (months 7–9)

- [ ] Prepare 4–5 STAR interview stories, at least 2 from homelab/C+D work
- [ ] Practice system design interviews 2x/week
- [ ] Begin coding interview prep (LeetCode easy/medium, focus on data structures + patterns)

### Visibility check — by month 6 you should see:

- [ ] 2–3 recruiter messages per week on LinkedIn
- [ ] Profile views increasing month-over-month
- [ ] At least 1 inbound from a company on your target list
