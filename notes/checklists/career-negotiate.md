## Notes

- Negotiation principle: whoever names a number first loses leverage. Get the company to state a range before you do.
- Total comp factors beyond base: equity/stock options (vesting schedule), annual bonus, remote equipment + learning budget, pension, healthcare.
- If staying at AlmavivA: frame as scope expansion ("I've been doing X, Y, Z which are senior responsibilities — I'd like the title and comp to match"). Bring market data.
